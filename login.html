FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is INVOICE HUBcompliant with latest GST rules?
Yes!Invoice Hub is fully compliant with the latest GST regulations including e-invoicing for businesses with turnover above ₹5 Crore, GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, QR code mandate, and IRN generation via NIC portal integration.
Can I migrate data from Tally or Zoho Books?
Absolutely. Invoice Hub supports bulk data import via Excel and CSV formats. Our migration team also provides free white-glove migration assistance for Professional and Enterprise plans.
Is my data secure?
We use bank-grade AES-256 encryption, ISO 27001 certified data centers in India, automated daily backups, and are fully DPDP Act compliant. Your data never leaves Indian servers.
Does it work offline?
Invoice Hub has a Progressive Web App (PWA) mode that lets you create invoices offline. Data syncs automatically when you reconnect. Our Android and iOS apps also support offline mode.
